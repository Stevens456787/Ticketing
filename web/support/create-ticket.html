Create a Support Ticket

Share details about your issue so the agency IT desk can respond quickly.

  1. Visit the agency ticket portal at support.your-agency.gov/tickets.
  2. Sign in with your agency credentials and choose Traffic Tickets from the product list.
  3. Provide a concise summary, attach screenshots if available, and include your officer badge or clerk ID.
  4. Submit the ticket. You will receive an email confirmation with a reference number.
