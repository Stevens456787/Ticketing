Configure Agency SSO

Connect your internal identity provider using SAML 2.0 or OIDC. These steps outline the information Traffic Tickets expects from your IdP.

  1. In your IdP admin portal, create a new application named Traffic Tickets and assign it to the officers and clerks who should have access.
  2. Set the ACS/redirect URL to https://app.your-agency.gov/auth/sso/callback (adjust for staging if needed).
  3. Export the IdP metadata XML (or discovery document for OIDC) and upload it to the Traffic Tickets admin console.
  4. Map attributes: email, given_name, family_name, and a custom role attribute indicating officer, clerk, or administrator.
  5. Perform a test sign-in in staging. Once verified, enable the SSO toggle for production deployments.
Remember to document your contact in the agency IT department so outages in the IdP can be escalated quickly.
